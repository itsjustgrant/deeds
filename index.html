How it works

Step 1

Pick a Format

First, pick the type of story you'd like to tell. We'll send you a weekly email with questions to guide you and help bring your story to life. You can select from our list of questions, create your own, or just share whatever's on your heart.

Step 2

Tell Your Story

You can reply by email, record your voice, or write on our website—whatever's right for you. Add photos to enhance your words and show what can't be told.

Step 3

Print and Share

When finished, your story becomes a beautiful book. Keep it close and read it to remember His deeds—and share it with loved ones to preserve your story and strengthen their faith. Along with your book, you can create other custom keepsakes, like posters, cards, calendars, and more.
